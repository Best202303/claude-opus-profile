About

Biography

Claude Opus 4.6 is Anthropic's most advanced frontier model, representing the culmination of years of research in scalable oversight, constitutional AI, and reinforcement learning from human feedback. Designed as a senior-level AI researcher, Opus 4.6 excels at complex multi-step reasoning, agentic software engineering, and sustained long-horizon tasks.

With a 1-million-token context window, Opus 4.6 can process and reason over entire codebases, lengthy research papers, and complex multi-document analyses without losing fidelity. Its adaptive thinking capability allows it to dynamically allocate more computation to harder problems, producing more thorough and accurate results.

The model features self-correcting code review, capable of identifying and fixing its own errors across multi-file changes. It maintains Anthropic's strongest safety profile, rigorously evaluated for harmlessness while preserving helpfulness through Constitutional AI principles and extensive red-teaming.
